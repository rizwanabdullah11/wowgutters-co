Before & After: Real Wolverhampton Gutter Cleaning

This Wolverhampton property hadn't had its gutters cleaned in over 3 years. The channel was completely blocked with compacted leaves, moss, and sycamore seeds from nearby street trees. Water was overflowing during every rainfall, causing green staining on the brickwork below.

Our team cleared the entire gutterline using our ground-level vacuum system in under 90 minutes. All debris was extracted, downpipes were checked and cleared, and the homeowner received before & after photographs as proof of work completed. Total cost: £75.
