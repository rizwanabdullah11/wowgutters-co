# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-28*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** WOW Gutters provides fast, safe, ladder-free gutter cleaning and roofline services with quick online quotes and same-day booking.

**What it does:** The site markets and sells residential + commercial exterior cleaning services (gutter cleaning, repairs, roof cleaning, fascia/soffit, downpipes, inspections). The primary conversion is getting a **FREE quote** via an on-site quote flow (static export) that submits to Web3Forms.

**Product category:** Local home services (gutter cleaning / roofline services / exterior cleaning).

**Product type:** Service business website (static Next.js export).

**Business model:** Per-job service pricing; lead capture via quote request (phone/email follow-up and booking).

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Homeowners/landlords; property managers; facilities managers (commercial).

**Decision-makers:** Homeowners; landlords; facilities/property managers; small business owners.

**Primary use case:** Get gutters cleared/cleaned quickly and safely to prevent blockages, leaks, and property damage.

**Jobs to be done:**
- Keep rainwater drainage working (avoid overflow, damp, structural issues)
- Maintain property appearance (roofline/UPVC/conservatory cleaning)
- Get a trustworthy local team with fast turnaround and clear pricing

**Use cases:**
- Seasonal gutter clearance (spring/autumn)
- Emergency downpipe blockage / overflowing gutters
- Pre-sale / pre-rent property cleanup
- Commercial building maintenance scheduling

## Personas
| Persona | Cares about | Challenge | Value we promise |
|---------|-------------|-----------|------------------|
| Homeowner | Speed, trust, price clarity | Hard to find reliable pros; safety worries | Fast quote + same-day booking; safe vacuum system |
| Landlord | Reliability, documentation, scheduling | Coordinating access; recurring maintenance | Repeatable process + quick response |
| Facilities/Property Manager | Compliance, scale, service coverage | Multi-site coordination; vendor management | Professional service + clear scope |

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Blocked/dirty gutters cause overflow, leaks, damp, and expensive damage.

**Why alternatives fall short:**
- DIY is unsafe (ladders), time-consuming, and inconsistent
- Some vendors have long lead times, unclear pricing, poor communication

**What it costs them:** Repairs, callouts, stress during heavy rain, reputational issues for property managers.

**Emotional tension:** Anxiety when water overflows; fear of being overcharged; uncertainty about who to trust.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Local gutter cleaning companies — vary in quality, speed, professionalism.

**Secondary:** General handymen / window cleaners — may not have equipment for tall buildings.

**Indirect:** DIY / “leave it until it’s bad” — high risk.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Ladder-free, ground-level vacuum system (safe)
- Quick online quote flow + fast response
- Same-day booking (positioning)
- 1-year guarantee (where applicable)

**Why customers choose us:** Fast, safe, reliable service with clear next steps (free quote → booking).

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| “How much will it cost?” | Free quote; clear scope; explain what affects price (height/storeys, access, work needed) |
| “Will you damage my property?” | Safety-first process; professional equipment; insured (add proof if available) |
| “Can you do it soon?” | Same-day booking where possible; quick response promise |

**Anti-persona:** Customers looking for the absolute cheapest option regardless of quality/safety.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Previous unreliable vendors; slow responses; unsafe practices.

**Pull:** Fast quote + fast booking; safe approach; trust signals (reviews/awards).

**Habit:** “We always use the same guy”; procrastination until there’s a problem.

**Anxiety:** Worried about cost, access, mess, and whether it’ll actually fix the issue.

## Customer Language
**How they describe the problem:**
- “My gutters are overflowing”
- “Downpipe is blocked”
- “Water’s running down the walls”
- “Need gutter cleaning price”

**Words to use:** free quote, same-day booking, safe, ladder-free, reliable, professional, guaranteed.

**Words to avoid:** overly technical terms without explanation.

## Brand Voice
**Tone:** Direct, reassuring, professional.

**Style:** Benefits-first; clear next steps; local trust emphasis.

**Personality:** Reliable, fast, safety-focused, friendly.

**Testimonials:** Use the existing Reviews section/page; add attributed quotes where possible.
